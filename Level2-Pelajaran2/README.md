**“Bertanya pada orang di jalan”** = “To ask someone on the street” (usually directions or information).

- **Bertanya** = “to ask a question”
    
    - Prefix **ber-** → indicates doing an action.
        
    - Root **tanya** → “ask / question.”
        
- **pada** = “to” (preposition, used when referring to people).
    
- **orang** = “person / people.”
    
- **di** = “at / in / on” (preposition for location).
    
- **jalan** = “road / street.”

Teguh and Satrio want to go to _Perusahaan Maju_ (Maju Company). On the way, they ask a traffic police officer for directions.

At the bus stop:

- **Satrio**: Guh, which road goes to Maju Company from here?
    
- **Teguh**: Hmm, I don’t really know. Let’s ask that policeman. Excuse me, Sir. Which way to Maju Company?
    
- **Police**: Oh, from here just go straight on Pajajaran Street, until Senopati Street. There, turn right, keep going on Senopati Street until Bintang Street. There, turn left, keep going on Bintang Street. Maju Company is on the left.
    
- **Teguh**: Sir, is it far from here to that company?
    
- **Police**: Oh, not far. About 15 minutes on foot.
    
- **Teguh**: Thank you, Sir. Satrio, let’s walk there.
    
- **Satrio**: By the way, Teguh, do you have any plans this Sunday?
    
- **Teguh**: Not sure yet. Maybe I’ll play badminton or soccer. Want to join?
### **Vocabulary and usage**

- **mau** = want (used casually, common in daily conversation).
    
- **pergi** = to go.
    
- **perusahaan** = company (root: _usaha_ = effort/business, with prefix _per-_ + suffix _-an_ = institution).
    
- **jalan** = road / to walk (depends on context).
    
- **tanya** = to ask.
    
- **polisi** = police.
    
- **permisi** = excuse me (polite).
    
- **belok** = to turn.

- **“Sebelah”** = “side” / “next to” / “beside.”
    
- **sebelah kiri** = left side.
    
- **jauh** = far.
    
- **kira-kira** = about / approximately.
    
- **terima kasih** = thank you.
    
- **ayo** = let’s (casual).
    
- **ngomong-ngomong** = by the way (casual).
    
- **acara** = event/plan.
    
- **belum tahu** = don’t know yet.
    
- **mungkin** = maybe.
    
- **main** = to play (games/sports).
    
- **sepak bola** = soccer (literally “kick ball”).
    

---

### **4. Word breakdown**

- **perusahaan** = _per-_ (prefix: indicates noun form) + _usaha_ (root: effort/business) + _-an_ (suffix: noun marker). → company.
    
- **jalan kaki** = _jalan_ (walk/road) + _kaki_ (foot). → to walk.
    
- **kira-kira** = reduplication → about/approximately.
    
- **ngomong-ngomong** = reduplication of _ngomong_ (to talk/speak) → by the way.
    
- **sepak bola** = _sepak_ (kick) + _bola_ (ball).
